# Whitepaper Outline: Advancing Open Standards & Interoperability in AEC

## I. Executive Summary
- The stagnation of foodservice design tools.
- The cost of technical silos and proprietary data.

## II. Technical Debt in Foodservice Design
- Proprietary formats as a barrier to multi-platform choice.
- Performance bottlenecks in modern BIM environments.
- The "Data Lag" problem: Why models are never up-to-date.

## III. The Pharos Solution: Open Data & High Performance
- Unified Metadata Standard (PKD JSON).
- Direct-to-Manufacturer data integrity.
- LOD (Level of Development) efficiency without metadata loss.

## IV. Case Study: Bridged Interoperability
- Seamless transition from legacy reports to live, normalized data.
- Achieving 100% schedule parity through **Interface Normalization**.

## V. Conclusion: The Future is Open
- Empowering the Independent Kitchen Designer (IKD).
- Call to Action: Join the Open Data revolution in Kitchen Design.
