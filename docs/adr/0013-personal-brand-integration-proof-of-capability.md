# ADR 0013: Personal Brand Integration & Proof of Capability

## Context
The project serves a dual purpose: disrupting legacy AEC silos and serving as a high-fidelity "Proof of Capability" for the lead engineer. It must be integrated with the primary professional hub (iamrichardd.com) to maximize marketability.

## Decision
Treat **Personal Brand Integration** as a first-class architectural requirement.
1.  **Direct Traceability**: Every PKD project highlight on `iamrichardd.com` must link directly to the **ADR Index** in this repository.
2.  **Meta-Architect Role**: Document the lead engineer's role as the **Pharos Meta-Architect**, emphasizing the management of multi-agent AI engineering workflows.
3.  **Live Validation**: The marketing site will feature a "Project Health" section visualizing live DORA metrics (Lead Time, Deployment Frequency) to demonstrate engineering maturity.

## Rationale
Investors and recruiters look for "Depth of Thought" and "Operational Maturity." By forcing visibility into the ADRs and DORA metrics, we prove that the lead engineer is not just writing code, but building **Deterministic Infrastructure** with a rigorous, scalable methodology.

## Impact
- The marketing site implementation must include a dashboard for DORA metrics.
- The `iamrichardd.com` site will be updated to reflect the PMA role and the PKD mission.
- The project becomes a case study in high-performance, AI-driven bootstrap engineering.
